The Gallery


Art Vault is a brand created by Alex Lopez that expresses his creativity through clothing.


Currently, Alex's pieces can be found in the Top Shop on 25 North 4th Street, Harrisburg, PA.


Art Vault also has a vlog channel, following the process of making clothes and the day to day life of Alex.
